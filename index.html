About AmanahYouth

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower youth by promoting active participation, community engagement, and personal growth. Through various programs, projects, and initiatives, we encourage young people to become responsible, compassionate, and productive members of society.

Our Vision

To build a generation of empowered youth leaders guided by faith, excellence, and service.

Organization Mission

To empower Amanah Youth through leadership development, community service, education, and meaningful engagement for a stronger and more united future.

Core Values

  • Faith
  • Unity
  • Service
  • Excellence

Motto

Empowering Amanah Youth Through Amanah and Leadership
