About AmanahYouth

Who We Are

AmanahYouth is a youth-led movement dedicated to fostering leadership, service, unity, and responsibility among young people. We believe in the power of collective action and transparent governance.

Our Core Values

  • Leadership through example and integrity
  • Service to our community and nation
  • Unity across all backgrounds and beliefs
  • Responsibility in every decision we make
  • Trust as the foundation of everything
